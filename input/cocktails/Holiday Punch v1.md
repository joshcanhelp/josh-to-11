
[[Large Batch]]

* 1 pint [[Brandy]]
* 2 bottles dry [[Sparkling Wine]]
* 1 cup grenadine
* 0.5 liter ginger ale/beer
* 1 pint sherbert
* 1 pkg frozen raspberries

**Steps:**

1. Add sparkling wine + soda slowly to a large punch bowl
1. Add grenadine + brandy
1. Then sherb
1. Float raspberries on top
