[[Large Batch]]

- 15 oz pineapple juice
- 7.5 oz lemon juice
- 5 oz vanilla syrup
- 10 oz [[Bourbon]]
- 10 oz aged [[Rum]]
- 5 oz [[Pear Liqueur]]
- 2.5 oz [[Allspice Liqueur]]
- Garnish: ground cinnamon

**Steps:**

1. Combine all ingredients into a pitcher
2. Cover and refrigerate for 4 hours
3. Pour into punch bowl with large chunks of ice
