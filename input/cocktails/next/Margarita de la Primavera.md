- 1.5 oz blanco [[Tequila]]
- 0.5 oz [[Campari]]
- 0.5 oz agave syrup
- 0.75 oz lime juice
- 1 large strawberry
- 3 dashes rhubarb bitters
- Garnish: lime wheel

**Steps:**

1. Rim glass with 50/50 sugar + salt
2. Muddle strawberry and agave
3. Add remaining ingredients and shake with ice
4. Strain into glass with ice
