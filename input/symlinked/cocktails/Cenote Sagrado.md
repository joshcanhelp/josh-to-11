* Splash of [[Mezcal]]
* 2 oz blanco [[Tequila]]
* 0.75 oz lime juice
* 0.75 oz [Modelo syrup](/cocktails/modelo-syrup-ingredient)
* 4 oz IPA

**Steps:**

1. Rinse a wine glass with mezcal
1. Combine tequila, lime, and syrup with ice and shake
1. Strain into wine glass and top with IPA
