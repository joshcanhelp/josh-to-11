Liber & Co makes [an amazing orgeat syrup](https://www.liberandcompany.com/products/almond-orgeat-syrup).

* 1 oz [[Gin]]
* 0.5 oz [[Orgeat]]
* 0.5 oz lemon juice
* 1 dash Angostura
* 4 oz blonde ale

**Steps:**

1. Shake everything except beer with ice and strain over ice
1. Top with beer
