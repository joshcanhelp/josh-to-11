- 1 oz [[Scotch]] infused with Lemon and Ginger
- 1 oz [[Cherry Heering]]
- 1 oz lemon juice
- 1 oz [[Sweet Vermouth]]
- Lemon Bitters
- 1 Egg White
- Garnish: lemon twist

**Steps:**

1. Dry shake all ingredients for 10 seconds
2. Add ice and vigorously shake for 20 seconds
3. Fine strain into cocktail glass and garnish with a lemon twist
